About Us

we stand as a beacon of unity, diversity, and collaboration within our school community. Our club is more than just an organization; it's a vibrant community where students come together to foster friendships, celebrate diversity, and create positive change.

UNITE LEARNING PROGRAM

To break the vicious cycle of poverty, oppression, and wasted human potential by empowering uniquely talented yet impoverished youth across Tanzania with access to education through A-Levels, university, and beyond, and — through our engaged team of international advisors and mentors — nurture creative and independent thinking and the development of personal and professional life skills necessary for success as active, engaged, and productive global citizens.

WHAT WE BELIEVE.

  • In UNITING people across cultures and disciplines to co-create effective solutions to the world’s problems.
  • In gender equality and the critical importance of quality education.
  • In the power of collaboration, mentorship, professionalism and volunteerism.
